Welcome to Lebon-Bon Inc

About Us

Hi there! We are Lebon-Bon Inc, a company dedicated to creating cool stuff. Our current project is all about making cold packs!

Project Overview

Imagine you have a magical substance that can make things super cold just by adding water. That's what we're exploring with potassium chloride! We want to turn it into a fantastic cold pack that helps when you get hurt.

Message for Mrs. Peake

Hey Mrs. Peake! This project took a lot of work, and we're hoping it's good enough for a great grade. We're giving it our best shot!

Project Steps

Here's what we're doing:

  1. Research: We're learning everything we can about potassium chloride and how it gets cold.
  2. Testing: We're trying out different mixes to see which one makes the best cold pack.
  3. Design: We're creating prototypes of our cold packs using safe and strong materials.
  4. Testing Again: We're putting our cold packs through lots of tests to make sure they work well and are safe.
  5. Improvement: If something isn't right, we'll fix it until we have the perfect cold pack!
